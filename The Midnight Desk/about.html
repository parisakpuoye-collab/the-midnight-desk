What is The Midnight Desk?

The Midnight Desk is a storybook world crafted from my late nights and the caves of my imagination. I'll keep adding stories to this site as I continue to build my universe.

My name is D.P Anansi, and I’m the creator of this world. With the dream of being a writer this is the closest I can get to sharing my stories with the world. I hope you enjoy reading them as much as I enjoy writing them.
